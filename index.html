ABOUT THE ARTIST

The Journey & Vision

I am Mansi, someone who perhaps took learning art in school a little too seriously. What began as discipline became devotion; art was never casual, but studied, practiced, and eventually loved. That seriousness built the foundation.

On the contrary, the name SNIPPET AND SPLOTCH does not take itself too seriously, intentionally so. For me, picking up a brush is about delight, not perfection; about lightness and play. Here, creation goes beyond a basic canvas. Different surfaces and mediums set Snippet and Splotch apart, with textures, layers, and mixed techniques shaping each story. The work is not just painted; it is built and explored. The process matters as much as the outcome.

For me, art is expression in its most vivid, radiant, viscerally visual form. It becomes rhythm, instinct, language — simply joy made visible.

"Picking up a brush is about delight, not perfection."

STORIES BEHIND THE ART

The Inspiration & Process

VIEW FULL SIZE

BLACK GARDEN

Floral forms emerge against a deep black background, creating a striking contrast and quiet intensity. This piece is made using water-soluble colour pencils, first activated with water for a soft, painterly base. Once dry, finer details were layered using the pencils dry, allowing precision and depth through a thoughtful dual technique.

VIEW FULL SIZE

JUNGLE ROYALTY

An exploration of strength and subtlety. Emerging from a tangle of forest florals, the leopard's form is rendered primarily in bold black outlines, with fine touches of gold adding quiet luminosity. Created on glass using nail paint liners and set against a white backing, the piece reimagines both medium and frame.

VIEW FULL SIZE

AFTER VAN GOGH

This acrylic interpretation of a self-portrait by Vincent van Gogh captures his iconic grey felt hat and the vivid blue-green tones that define his palette. Carefully observed and faithfully rendered, the piece echoes his signature short, expressive brushstrokes. It stands as a close study — an homage shaped through attentive understanding of his technique and detail.

VIEW FULL SIZE

GOLDEN MANDALA

This hand-built clay mandala rises beyond the flat surface, sculpted layer by layer with careful precision. Finished in luminous gold spray paint, the 3D piece stands independently with a handmade backing. Subtle fairy lights placed behind it cast delicate shadows, transforming it into a glowing mantle display that blends sculpture, craft, and quiet illumination.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything You Need to Know

HOW DO I PURCHASE AN ARTWORK?

Simply contact me via email at snippetandsplotch@gmail.com or send a direct message on Instagram @snippetandsplotch with the artwork title you're interested in. I'll respond within 24 hours with availability, payment options, and shipping details.

DO YOU SHIP INTERNATIONALLY?

Yes! I ship worldwide. All artworks are carefully packaged to ensure they arrive safely. Shipping costs vary by location and will be calculated based on your address. International orders typically arrive within 7-14 business days.

ARE THE ARTWORKS FRAMED?

Most artworks are sold unframed to allow you to choose framing that matches your space. However, I can recommend local framers or provide framing services for an additional fee. Each piece comes with care instructions and framing suggestions.

DO YOU ACCEPT COMMISSIONS?

Yes! I love creating custom pieces. Commission work typically requires a 50% deposit upfront, with the remainder due upon completion. The process includes initial consultations, sketches for approval, and regular updates. Timeline varies by project complexity, usually 2-6 weeks.

WHAT'S YOUR RETURN POLICY?

I want you to love your artwork! If you're not completely satisfied, you may return it within 14 days of delivery for a full refund (minus shipping costs). The artwork must be in its original condition. Custom commissioned pieces are non-refundable.

IS EACH PIECE AN ORIGINAL OR A PRINT?

Each artwork listing clearly states whether it's an original or a print. Original pieces are one-of-a-kind and come with a certificate of authenticity. Prints are high-quality reproductions, often in limited editions. All originals are signed and dated on the back.

HOW DO I CARE FOR MY ARTWORK?

Each artwork comes with specific care instructions based on its medium. Generally, keep artwork away from direct sunlight, excessive humidity, and heat. For paper-based art, use UV-protective glass when framing. Dust gently with a soft, dry cloth. Never use cleaning products directly on the artwork.

CAN I SEE THE ARTWORK IN PERSON BEFORE BUYING?

While I don't have a permanent gallery space, I occasionally participate in local art shows and open studio events. Follow me on Instagram @snippetandsplotch for announcements. I'm also happy to provide additional photos or video of specific pieces upon request.

DO YOU OFFER GIFT CERTIFICATES?

Yes! Gift certificates are available in any amount and make perfect gifts for art lovers. They can be used toward any artwork or custom commission. Contact me directly to arrange a gift certificate purchase.
